How to Save & Share This Invitation

Follow these simple steps to create your invitation link and share it with guests

1

Save the File

On Computer: Press Ctrl+S (Windows) or Cmd+S (Mac)
On Phone: Tap "Share" → "Save to Files"

2

Upload Online

Upload the saved file to:
• Google Drive
• Dropbox
• GitHub Pages (free)
• Any web hosting service

3

Get Your Link

Once uploaded, get the public link to your file.
Example:
https://yourname.github.io/ruru-christening
